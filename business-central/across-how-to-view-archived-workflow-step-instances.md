## <a name="to-view-archived-workflow-step-instances"></a><span data-ttu-id="7e32f-109">Gearchiveerde instanties van werkstroomstappen bekijken</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e32f-109">To view archived workflow step instances</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="7e32f-111">Open de werkstroom waarvoor u alle instanties van gearchiveerde werkstroomstappen wilt bekijken.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e32f-111">Open the workflow for which you want to view all archived workflow step instances.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="7e32f-113">De pagina **Gearchiveerde werkstroomstapinstanties** opent met de gearchiveerde werkstroomstapinstanties van de geselecteerde werkstroom.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e32f-113">The **Archived Workflow Step Instances** page opens showing the archived workflow step instances of the selected workflow.</span></span>

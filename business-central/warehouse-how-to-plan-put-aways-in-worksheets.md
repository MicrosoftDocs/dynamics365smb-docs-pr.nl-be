Ga hiertoe als volgt te werk:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6097b-108">Regardless of the status of the **Use Put-away Worksheet** field on the location card, you can always get put-away instruction lines, that is, posted receipt lines, into the put-away worksheet by doing the following:</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="6097b-116">Dit venster bevat alle geboekte ontvangsten en geregistreerde interne opslagactiviteiten die zijn doorgestuurd naar de opslagfunctie, inclusief de activiteiten waarvoor de opslaginstructies al zijn gemaakt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6097b-116">You see all the posted receipts and registered internal put-aways that have been forwarded to the put-away function, including those for which put-away instructions have already been created.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6097b-117">De lijst bevat geen documenten met opslagregels die volledig zijn uitgevoerd en geregistreerd.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6097b-117">Documents with put-away lines that have been completely put away and registered are not shown on this list.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]

    -   <span data-ttu-id="6097b-137">U kunt het veld **Te verwerken aantal** op de opslaginstructies automatisch laten invullen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6097b-137">You can choose not to have the **Qty. to Handle** field automatically filled in on the put-away instructions.</span></span>  
    -   <span data-ttu-id="6097b-138">Indien gewenst kunt u het document direct afdrukken.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6097b-138">You can choose to print the document immediately.</span></span>

## <a name="to-post-capacities"></a><span data-ttu-id="c0949-107">Capaciteit boeken</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c0949-107">To post capacities</span></span>  
1.  <span data-ttu-id="c0949-108">Klik op het pictogram ![Zoeken naar pagina of rapport](media/ui-search/search_small.png ""pictogram Zoeken naar pagina of rapport"), voer **Capaciteitsdagboeken** in en klik vervolgens op de gerelateerde koppeling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c0949-108">Choose the ![Search for Page or Report](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Search for Page or Report icon") icon, enter **Capacity Journals**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>  
2.  <span data-ttu-id="c0949-109">Vul de velden **Boekingsdatum** en **Documentnr.** in.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c0949-109">Fill in the **Posting Date** and **Document No.** fields.</span></span>  
3.  <span data-ttu-id="c0949-110">Voer in het veld **Soort** het soort capaciteit in dat u wilt boeken, dat kan **Bewerkingsplaats** of **Afdeling** zijn.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c0949-110">In the **Type** field, enter the type of the capacity, either **Machine Center** or **Work Center**, that you are posting.</span></span>  
4.  <span data-ttu-id="c0949-111">Selecteer in het veld **Nr.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c0949-111">In the **No.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c0949-112">het nummer in van de bewerkingsplaats of afdeling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c0949-112">field, enter the number of the machine center or work center.</span></span>  
5.  <span data-ttu-id="c0949-113">Voer in de andere velden de relevante gegevens in, bijvoorbeeld **Begintijd**, **Eindtijd**, **Aantal** en **Uitval**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c0949-113">Enter the relevant data in the other fields, such as **Starting Time**, **Ending Time**, **Quantity**, and **Scrap**.</span></span>  
6.  <span data-ttu-id="c0949-114">Kies de actie **Boeken** om de capaciteit te boeken.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c0949-114">Choose the **Post** action to post the capacities.</span></span>  

## <a name="to-view-work-center-ledger-entries"></a><span data-ttu-id="c0949-115">Afdelingsposten weergeven</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c0949-115">To view work center ledger entries</span></span>
